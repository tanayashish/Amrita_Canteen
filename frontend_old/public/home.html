About Amrita Canteen

Amrita Canteen is a revolutionary smart dining platform designed specifically for campus life. Built by students, for students, we understand the challenges of long queues, limited break times, and unpredictable food availability.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with traditional campus dining to create a seamless, efficient, and enjoyable experience. Using machine learning for demand forecasting, real-time order tracking, and a mobile-first design, we're transforming how students interact with their canteen.

Our Mission

To eliminate wait times, reduce food waste, and make delicious, affordable meals accessible to every student on campus through smart technology and thoughtful design.
